Hi, I'm @solvaholic.

I need a problem to solve in much the same way an alcoholic needs a drink. That's what drives the writing here and the software I build - curiosity, an itch to understand how things work, and a compulsion to fix what's broken.

A few things I try to live by:

- Seek opportunities to verify or correct what you know and believe. [Being wrong feels exactly like being right.](https://www.ted.com/talks/kathryn_schulz_on_being_wrong)
- We're each working with different, incomplete, and imperfect information. If you're going to fill in blanks, tell a story you enjoy.
- If a [serial comma](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Serial_comma) eliminates ambiguity, use it.
- Sign your name.

> *Truth is the edges of what you pay attention to.*
